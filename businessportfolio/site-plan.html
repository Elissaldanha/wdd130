Purpose

The purpose of this personal website is to provide comprehensive web design and development services tailored specifically for small business owners. It will offer a range of customizable website solutions, including design, development, and maintenance, to help small businesses establish a professional online presence and effectively showcase their products or services to a broader audience.

Audience

The intended audience for this website includes small business owners, entrepreneurs, startups, and individuals looking to establish or improve their online presence. It aims to cater to those who recognize the importance of a well-designed website in enhancing their brand visibility and attracting potential customers.
